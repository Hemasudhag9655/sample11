Mystical Forests

Discover the wild beauty of India’s forests and national parks.

Famous Forests

Sundarbans, West Bengal

Home of the Royal Bengal Tiger and largest mangrove forest in the world.

Jim Corbett National Park

India’s oldest national park — a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts.

Kaziranga, Assam

Home of the one-horned rhino and lush green plains of Assam.

Periyar National Park, Kerala

Famous for its scenic lake and elephant sightings amidst thick greenery.

Gir Forest, Gujarat

The only natural habitat of the Asiatic Lion in the world.

Bandhavgarh National Park, Madhya Pradesh

Known for its high density of Bengal tigers and rich biodiversity.

Silent Valley, Kerala

A pristine rainforest known for rare species and untouched wilderness.

Ranthambore, Rajasthan

Historic fort ruins within a dense forest, home to majestic tigers.
