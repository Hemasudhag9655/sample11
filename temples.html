Ancient Temples of India

Marvels of devotion, architecture, and centuries of faith.

Famous Ancient Temples

Brihadeeswarar Temple, Tamil Nadu

A UNESCO World Heritage site built by Raja Raja Chola I, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

Famous for its intricate stone carvings and chariot-shaped structure symbolizing the Sun God’s journey.

Kedarnath Temple, Uttarakhand

One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites nestled amidst snow-clad Himalayan peaks.

Meenakshi Temple, Tamil Nadu

Located in Madurai, this temple is known for its towering gopurams and colorful sculptures.

Somnath Temple, Gujarat

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, rebuilt several times through history.

Jagannath Temple, Puri

One of the Char Dham sites, famous for its annual Rath Yatra dedicated to Lord Jagannath.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi

Located on the banks of the Ganga, it’s one of the most sacred temples of Lord Shiva.

Virupaksha Temple, Hampi

A part of the Hampi Group of Monuments, dedicated to Lord Shiva and known for its stunning carvings.
