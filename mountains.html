Majestic Mountains

Feel the chill of the Himalayas and experience serenity like never before.

Famous Mountain Destinations

Nainital, Uttarakhand

Known as the “Lake District of India”, famous for boating, hills, and scenic views.

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Once the British summer capital, famous for its colonial charm and mountain views.

Leh-Ladakh

A paradise for bikers and adventure seekers, with monasteries and snow-capped peaks.

Munnar, Kerala

Known for rolling tea plantations and misty hills in the Western Ghats.

Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

Home to the majestic Tawang Monastery and breathtaking Himalayan views.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Famous for its tea gardens and stunning views of the Kanchenjunga range.

Gangtok, Sikkim

A vibrant hill town with views of Kanchenjunga and colorful Buddhist culture.

Mount Abu, Rajasthan

Rajasthan’s only hill station, known for the Dilwara Temples and Nakki Lake.
